Meet the Chef

The heart and soul behind Food by K

Chef Koketjo Semenya

Founder & Head Chef

With a passion for authentic flavors and a love for his community, Chef Koketjo brings the heart of the kasi to every dish. His culinary journey is built on the belief that food should bring people together, nourish the soul, and celebrate our rich culture.

Featured on Radio 2000's Several Colours, Chef K continues to inspire with his dedication to quality and his commitment to serving food made with love.
